About the job

Founding Software Engineer - Equity Based
One Frequency | MusicTech | AI/ML Innovation

Location: Remote

Type: Founding Team Role - Equity Based

Industry: Music Technology, Enterprise SaaS, AI/ML

About One Frequency

One Frequency is revolutionizing the music industry through cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies. We’re building enterprise SaaS solutions that transform how music is created, distributed, and consumed. As a MusicTech innovator, we’re positioned at the intersection of artificial intelligence and musical creativity.

The Role

We’re seeking a founding software engineer to join our core team and help build the next generation of music technology. This is a unique opportunity to work directly with seasoned technology leaders while having significant impact on product architecture and technical direction from day one.

What You’ll Do

Frontend Development: Build responsive, performant React applications with TypeScript for enterprise music industry clients
Full Stack Architecture: Design and implement scalable backend systems and APIs that power our AI-driven music platform
Cloud Infrastructure: Deploy and manage applications across AWS, GCP, and Azure environments
DevOps Excellence: Build robust CI/CD pipelines and automated deployment processes
Agile Development: Work in fast-paced sprints with direct customer feedback loops
Technical Innovation: Contribute to architectural decisions and help define our technology stack
Product Collaboration: Work closely with product and design teams to deliver exceptional user experiences

What You Need

Core Technical Skills:
5+ years of software development experience
Expert-level React and TypeScript proficiency
Strong backend development experience (Node.js, Python, or similar)
Solid understanding of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
Database experience (SQL and NoSQL)
API design and microservices architecture

Additional Strengths:
Solutions architecture experience
Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
Understanding of scalable system design
GraphQL or REST API development
Experience with testing frameworks and TDD
Agile/Scrum methodology experience

Startup Mindset:
Comfortable working in ambiguous, fast-moving environments
Ownership mentality with ability to drive projects to completion
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
Interest in music technology or creative industries is a plus

What We’re Looking For

Technical Excellence: Write clean, maintainable code that scales with our growing user base
Growth Mindset: Eager to learn new technologies and adapt to changing requirements
Team Player: Collaborative approach to building products and mentoring other engineers
Entrepreneurial Spirit: Think like an owner and contribute to business decisions
Quality Focus: Commitment to delivering high-quality software that delights users

What We Offer

Founding Team Equity: Significant ownership stake in a high-potential company
Technical Leadership: Direct influence on architecture and technology decisions
Cutting-Edge Work: Build AI-powered applications that transform the music industry
Learning Opportunities: Work alongside experienced technology leaders and industry experts
Remote Flexibility: Work from anywhere while building something extraordinary
Professional Growth: Opportunity to grow into senior technical leadership roles

Our Tech Stack

Frontend: React, TypeScript, modern CSS frameworks
Backend: Node.js, Python, GraphQL, REST APIs
Cloud: AWS, GCP, Azure (multi-cloud strategy)
Data: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis
DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines
AI/ML: Integration with cutting-edge music AI technologies

Our Values

We operate with Honor, Courage, and Commitment - values that drive both technical excellence and innovation. We believe in building technology that enhances human creativity and transforms entire industries.
